# Training Management - HR Quick Start Guide

**For HR Managers & Trainers**

---

## 🎯 What is Training Management?

The **Training Management Module** helps HR create training programs, assign them to employees based on their skill needs, track progress, and evaluate effectiveness.

---

## 📌 Quick Navigation

From HR2 Dashboard, look for **Training Management** in the left sidebar:

```
Training Management
├── Dashboard          ← Overview & statistics
├── Training Programs  ← Create/manage programs
├── Training Assignments  ← Assign to employees
└── Training Evaluation   ← Post-training feedback
```

---

## 🚀 Main Features

### 1. Training Dashboard
**What:** Overview of all training activity  
**Where:** Training → Dashboard  
**See:**
- Total training assignments
- Completion rate
- Employees with skill gaps
- Upcoming trainings
- Recommended trainings
- Average training hours

---

### 2. Create Training Programs
**What:** Define new training programs  
**Where:** Training → Training Programs → New Training Program  
**Steps:**
1. Enter **Training Name** (required)
2. Add **Description**
3. Select related **Competency** (e.g., "Emergency Response")
4. Choose **Required Skill Level** (1-5)
5. Select **Training Type**: Medical, Technical, or Soft Skills
6. Enter **Duration** in hours (required)
7. Add **Trainer/Instructor** name
8. Set **Schedule Date** (optional)
9. Click **Create Training Program**

**Result:** Training appears in list, ready to assign

---

### 3. Assign Training to Employees

#### Option A: Manual Assignment
**Steps:**
1. Go to Training → Training Assignments → Assign Training
2. Select **Manual Assignment** (default)
3. Choose a **Training Program** from dropdown
4. **Select Employees** - check boxes next to names
5. Click **Assign to X Employees**

**Result:** Training assigned, employees notified (future)

---

#### Option B: Smart Auto-Assignment ⭐
**What:** System automatically finds employees who need training based on skill gaps  
**Steps:**
1. Go to Training → Training Assignments → Assign Training
2. Click **Auto Assignment** card
3. Review **Affected Employees** (shows who needs training)
4. Click **Auto-Assign Trainings**

**Result:** Trainings automatically assigned to employees with skill gaps

---

### 4. Track Training Progress

**Status Options:**
- **Assigned** - Employee notified, hasn't started
- **In Progress** - Currently attending training
- **Completed** - Training finished
- **Missed** - Employee didn't attend
- **Cancelled** - Training cancelled

**How to Update:**
1. Go to Training → Training Assignments
2. Find assignment in list
3. Click **Status dropdown**
4. Select new status
5. Auto-saves

---

### 5. Evaluate Training Effectiveness

**When:** After training is completed  
**Where:** Training → Training Evaluation  
**Steps:**
1. Find completed training in list
2. Click **Evaluate** button
3. Rate overall performance (1-5 stars)
4. Rate **Knowledge Improvement** (1-5)
5. Rate **Performance Improvement** (1-5)
6. Add **Trainer Feedback** (comments)
7. Click **Save Evaluation**

**Ratings:**
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Excellent
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Good
- ⭐⭐⭐ Fair
- ⭐⭐ Poor
- ⭐ Very Poor

## 💡 Common Workflows

### Workflow 1: Hire New Nurse, Add Required Training
1. Employee hired in HR1 (status: "Employee Activated")
2. Open Training Management
3. Training → Training Programs → view "Emergency Response Level 3"
4. Training → Training Assignments → Manual
5. Select "Emergency Response Level 3"
6. Select new nurse
7. Assign

**Result:** Nurse has training assignment, can track progress

---

### Workflow 2: System Suggests Training for Skill Gap
1. Open Training → Dashboard
2. See "Identified Skill Gaps" section
3. See "Dr. Smith: Critical Care Level 2 → Level 3 (Gap: 1)"
4. Go to Training → Assignments → Auto Assignment
5. Click Auto-Assign
6. System assigns "Critical Care Advanced Training" to Dr. Smith

**Result:** Employee automatically assigned based on competency gap

---

### Workflow 3: Evaluate Training Completed by Employee
1. Training → Training Evaluation
2. Find "John Doe - Emergency Response Training - Completed"
3. Click Evaluate
4. Fill star ratings and feedback
5. Save

**Result:** Evaluation recorded, shows on dashboard

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Can I search for trainings?**  
A: Yes! On Training Programs page, use search box or filter by type (Medical, Technical, Soft Skills)

**Q: What if no employees need training?**  
A: Dashboard shows "0" for Employees with Gaps. You can still manually assign training.

**Q: Can I change a training after creating it?**  
A: Yes! Click Edit on training card to modify details.

**Q: What if I assigned wrong training?**  
A: Go to Assignments, click Remove on the assignment.

**Q: When do employees see their trainings?**  
A: Through Employee Portal (future feature). Currently only visible to HR.

**Q: Can training link to courses?**  
A: Yes! When creating training, you can link to Learning Management courses.
