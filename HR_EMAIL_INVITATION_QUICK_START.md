# 🚀 Email Invitation Quick Start - HR Users

## What's New?

When you schedule an interview with an applicant, **an email invitation is automatically sent to their email address** 📧

---

## How to Schedule an Interview with Email

### Step 1: Find an Applicant
- Go to **HR1 → Applicants**
- Click on the applicant you want to schedule an interview for

### Step 2: Click "Schedule Interview"
- Opens the interview scheduling dialog
- **You'll see the applicant's email at the top** ✓
- This confirms their email is on file

### Step 3: Fill in Interview Details

| Field | What to Enter | Required |
|-------|--------------|----------|
| 📅 Date | Interview date | Yes |
| ⏰ Time | Interview time | Yes |
| 🎯 Type | Online or On-site | Yes |
| 📍 Location OR 🔗 Link | Location for on-site, meeting link for online | Yes* |
| 👥 Panel | Select interviewers (optional) | No |
| 📝 Notes | Additional notes | No |

**\*Required based on interview type*

### Step 4: Click "Schedule Interview"
- System creates the interview record
- **Email automatically sends to applicant** 📬
- You'll see a success message

---

## What Applicant Sees

The applicant receives a **professional email** with:

✓ Interview date & time
✓ Interview type (online/on-site)
✓ Location or meeting link
✓ Tips for preparing for the interview
✓ HR contact information

## Troubleshooting

### ❌ "No Email Found" Message

**What it means**: Applicant doesn't have an email address on file

**Fix options**:
1. Add email to applicant's profile first
2. Contact HR to update applicant record
3. Schedule interview, then send email manually

### ⚠️ Text turns yellow when scheduling

**What it means**: Email failed to send, but interview was created

**What to do**:
1. Interview is still scheduled in the system
2. Reach out to applicant directly via phone
3. Contact IT if issue persists

### ✓ Green checkmark appears

**What it means**: Everything successful! 🎉
- Interview created
- Email sent to applicant

| ⚠️ Yellow box | Email couldn't be loaded |

## Best Practices

### ✅ Do's
- Check email is visible before scheduling
- Include relevant notes for the applicant
- Schedule interviews in advance when possible
- Keep interview panel informed

### ❌ Don'ts
- Don't change applicant's email after scheduling
- Don't assume email was received (it might bounce)
- Don't forget to add location/meeting link details
- Don't schedule outside business hours (emails may bounce)

2. Find the interview you just scheduled
3. Look for email status indicator

### If You Don't See Status

Contact IT with:
- Applicant name

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I resend the email?**
A: Not yet, but it's coming soon! For now, share the details with applicant directly.

**Q: What if applicant's email is wrong?**
A: Edit their profile, update the email, then schedule a new interview (we'll prevent duplicate emails soon).

**Q: Does applicant see I scheduled the interview?**
A: Yes! They get an email with full details.

**Q: Can applicants reply to the email?**
A: The email is automated. They should contact HR directly at hr@hospital.local for questions.
